London DJ Tommy Gold Partners with Seventh on New 'Coffee' V2 Collection.

Tommy Gold, West London DJ known for versatile sets and cultural diversity, fronts Seventh's latest 'Coffee' V2 tracksuit campaign. Shot at Young Space, the project highlights Tommy's journey from diverse roots and early challenges to collaborations with Louis Vuitton, Boiler Room, and NTS Radio.

At just 25, Tommy has earned recognition for his emotional performances and authentic approach. His Seventh partnership celebrates shared commitments to simplicity and universal appeal in both his musical style and the brand's core.

→ The 'Coffee' V2 tracksuit, with its deep chestnut tone
and 90s-inspired silhouette, drops Thursday, 1st February.
